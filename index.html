Phlea Farm is located in Alinda bay about 2 minutes walk from the sea.

It is home to around 500 very old trees – oranges, tangerines, lemons, guavas, olives – some of which are over 100 years old. Since 5 years now we started implementing the principles of Natural Farming according to Masanobu Fukuoka and Panagiotis Manikis on the farm. We aim at maximizing biodiversity and becoming a showcase for an alternative to mainstream agriculture.

The farm has 2 guest houses and a workshop space planted next to a small citrus grove of tangerines and oranges where guests can relax to the sounds of nature and enjoy the refreshing summer breezes. We invite you to explore our activities, accommodation and products, and plan your next trip close to nature!
